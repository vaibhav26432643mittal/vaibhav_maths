Cube

Cuboid

Recall a cuboid has six faces. All are rectangles.

Area of top and bottom faces = (lb+lb) cm2 = 2lb cm2

Area of front and back faces = (lh+lh) cm2 = 2lh cm2

Area of side faces = (bh + bh) cm2 = 2bh cm2

Total surface area = 2lb + 2lh + 2bh = 2(lb + bh + lh)
